【en】You are free to choose your own path.

【zh】笑到最后才笑得最好。

【en】He who laughs last laughs best.

【zh】积极的思考带来更多的快乐。

【en】Positive thinking initiates more happiness!

【zh】擅长的事情，绝不能白干。

【en】If you are good at something, never do it for free.

【zh】我们来自不同的世界，但我们彼此相容。

【en】We are from different worlds, yet we fit together.

【zh】你若盛开，蝴蝶自来。

【en】The better you become, the better you attract.

【zh】答应自己要坚强，不让任何事物烦扰内心的平静。

【en】Promise yourself to be so strong that nothing can disturb your peace of mind.

【zh】生活是一场大冒险。

【en】To live would be an awfully big adventure.

【zh】你可以随心所欲，但不能随波逐流。

【en】You can do whatever you want, but you can’t drift with the tide.

【zh】你定上火车票了吗？

【en】Have you booked your ticket?

**【zh】经历逆境会让你知道谁是真正的朋友。 **

【en】Adversity does teach you who your real friends are.

【zh】透过失败的迷雾，才能瞥见胜利的光辉。

【en】Failure is the fog through which we glimpse triumph.

【zh】别杞人忧天了，活在当下。

【en】Take a break from worrying about what you can’t control. Live a little.

【zh】有时候拒绝会让事情变得更好。

【en】Sometimes things go better when you just say no.

【zh】凡是成功就要付出代价。

【en】No victory comes without a price.

【zh】你的信任是我前进的动力。

【en】Your faith is all that keeps me going.

【zh】是好还是坏，只要做你自己就好。

【en】Be good. Be bad. Just be yourself.

【zh】不会捕鱼的人，只会把河水弄浑。

【en】Those who can’t fish will only muddy the river.

【zh】有些人因罪恶而升迁，有些人因美德而没落。

【en】Some rise by sin, and some by virtue fall.

【zh】诸邪退散！

【en】Kill the evil out!

【zh】我们都在负重前行。

【en】We all carry something with us.

【zh】是时候开始过自己想要的生活了！

【en】It’s time to start living the life you’ve imagined!

【zh】做好保暖，凛冬将至。

【en】Brace yourself. Winter is coming.

【zh】医生手册写道：开怀大笑，睡个好觉，此乃灵丹妙药 。

【en】A good laugh and a long sleep are the best cures in the doctor’s book.

【zh】怕什么？做梦就做的大一点儿！

【en】You mustn’t be afraid to dream a little bigger!

【zh】虎年行大运。

【en】Good luck in the Year of the Tiger.

【zh】不费力气，一无所得。

【en】Nothing for nothing.

【zh】应该抗议时却保持沉默，这种罪过使人成为懦夫。

【en】To sin by silence when we should protest makes cowards out of men.

【zh】爱意东升西落，浪漫至死不渝。

【en】Love rises in the east and falls in the west, romance lasts till death.

【zh】上帝借由各种途径使人变得孤独，好让我们可以走向自己。

【en】God makes people lonely in a variety of ways so that we can go to ourselves.

【zh】愿人生静好，岁岁含香。

【en】May life be quiet and sweet every year.

【zh】当一切都在走下坡时，要向上看。

【en】When things are looking down, look up.

【zh】人的生命是有限的，可是为人民服务是无限的。

【en】Human life is limited, and serving the people is infinite.

【zh】自律是自由的首要条件。

【en】Self-discipline is the first condition of freedom.
