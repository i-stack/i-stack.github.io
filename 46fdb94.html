中英对照治愈系文案

书生文案馆 文案 2023/03/13

1、像是漂浮在宇宙的行星，最终被银河牵引，只要终点是你，跨越再多光年我也愿意。
Like a planet floating in the universe, eventually pulled by the Milky Way, as long as the end is you, I would like to cross many light years、
2、我是银河里的流浪家，你是月球里的常驻民，我环游整个宇宙，只为找寻你的踪迹、
I am a wanderer in the Milky Way, you are a resident of the moon, I travel around the whole universe, just to find your trace、
3、时间美好有千千万万种，日出日落一种，星河大海一种，还有你的风情万种。
There are thousands of beautiful time, a sunrise and sunset, a sea of stars, there are thousands of your style、
4、在我的这颗小星球里面，你就是温柔跟璀璨，即使旁边的宇宙再浪漫，我也终身不换。
In my small planet inside, you are gentle and bright, even if the next to the universe again romantic, I will not change for life、
5、我是个俗气至顶的人，见山是山，见海是海，见花便是花。唯独见了你，云海开始翻涌，江潮开始澎湃，昆虫的小触须开始挠着全世界的痒。你无需开口，我和天地万物便通通奔向你。
I am a tacky person、 I see mountains as mountains, sea as sea, flowers as flowers、 Only seeing you, the sea of clouds began to surge, the river tide began to surging, and the tiny tentacles of insects began to tickle the world、 You don’t need to speak, I and the world and everything will run towards you、
每想你一次，我院子里的星星便多一颗，如今，那些零碎的欢喜，竟已凑成了银河
Every time I think of you, the stars in my yard will be more than one, now, those fragmentary joy, has actually made up the Milky Way、
7、“温柔的好天气总是和你一样 让人止不住心动”
“Gentle weather is always as exciting as you、”
8、“仍然有开在泥泞里温柔的花，有明晃晃的夏天 我喜欢这个世界 但更喜欢你”
“There are still soft flowers blooming in the mud, bright summer, I like the world, but I like you better、”
9、“十里清风 万顷星河 你和他们一样 都是小宝藏”
“You are as much a treasure as they are、”
10、“能够同时拥有可爱和温柔 把黑夜点亮的人都是神明 所以你是神明”
“He who can light up the night with loveliness and tenderness is a God, so you are a God、”
11、经历了世事的磨难，我依然觉得爱生活的人内心强大。
After the suffering of the world, I still feel that the person who loves life is strong inside、
12、我爱你，你也一定要爱我，这不是你的态度，而是生活的温情。
I like you, you must also like me, this is not your attitude, but also a kind of life gentle、
13、选择了一切，但我还是觉得你是我最应该珍惜的人。
To end everything I choose, I still think you are the one I should cherish the most、
14、我想看山海，我是说和你在一起，我觉得一切都是那样。
I want to see the mountains and seas, I mean with you, I think everything is like that、
15、好像温柔在追逐浪漫，而你只是在看着我。
It’s like gently chasing romance, and you just look at me、
16、在此之前，我也许会有一些敷衍和吵闹，但当我看到你时，我感觉世界充满了阳光。
I may have been a little perfunctory and noisy, but when I saw you, I think the world is full of sunshine、
17、想念那个时刻我不需要太多的主动，但我的细节你都懂。
I miss moments like that don’t need much initiative, but you all know my details、
18、我慢慢忘却童年最快乐的事时，我也慢慢忘却幼稚的单纯。
As time I slowly forgot the happiest things I had when I was a child, I also slowly forgot the childish simplicity、
19、有朝一日你会明白，适合比喜欢更重要。
One day you’ll understand that fit is more important than like it、
20、那一天肯定不会晚，总有一天会明白，合宜远比喜欢重要。
I’m sure it won’t be too late, and one day it will be more important to understand that fit is far more important than like it、
21、有很多东西你可以喜欢，但是只有那个适合你的人才适合你。
You can like a lot of things, but only fit but only that person、
22、所有人都希望有一天我可以和你们说说来时的路，但是时间似乎不给我这个机会。
I hope to come home, I can tell you the way, but time does not seem to give me such a chance、
23、人往前走，苦才会后退。
If you go forward, you will retreat、
24、我的每一支笔都知道你的名字。
Every pen of mine knows your name、
25、 就在一次次日落月升里，再爱一次人间。
In the sunset and moon rise, love the world again、
26、你买了瓶水，我也跟着拿，明明本来不渴。
You bought a bottle of water, and I took it with me、 It was clear that I was not thirsty、
27、总会相逢就像山川河流就像万河归海。
Always meet, just like mountains and rivers, like thousands of rivers return to the sea、
28、原来浮生万物里，所有重逢都不如相遇。
It turns out that in all living things, all reunion is better than meeting、
29、你就站在那里，什么都不用做，我就满心欢喜。
You just stand there and do nothing, and I’m happy、
30、不管我本人多么平庸，我就觉得我对你的爱很美。
No matter how mediocre I am, I feel my love for you is beautiful、
31、我对你的爱从春去秋来慢了又溢周而复始。
My love for you has been slow and overflowing from spring to autumn、
32、江山如画不如你眉眼清浅，山河千秋不及你泛笑风华。
Picturesque rivers and mountains are not as beautiful as your eyes and eyebrows、 Mountains and rivers are not as beautiful as you、
